About


My name is Brenda and I’m the person behind The Dormouse’s Desk. Prior to the pandemic, I was an avid bullet journaler, then the bottom dropped out and there just wasn’t anything left to organize in my life. Time spent doing elaborate monthly and weekly spreads turned into time reflecting and processing the state of the world. 


That’s when I fell hard into the joy of fountain pens and ink. Naturally, paper was sure to follow. Now I’m delightfully swimming in the most enjoyable stationery collection I can cultivate and sharing my art and my joy with all of you.
