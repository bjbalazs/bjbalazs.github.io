Montblanc- Enzo Ferrari Purple


Released in late 2021 alongside two new Great Characters pens, this ink is an homage to Enzo Ferrari. Mr. Ferrari’s favorite ink color was purple, and so, we have here a dark plum purple ink.


The ink has nice flow, not overly wet, which means it dries fairly quickly on normal papers (10 seconds when I tested it). The ink has some mild shading. Sheen only in heavy applications on papers like Tomoe River and Cosmo Air Light.


Overall, I think it’s a lovely ink and I’m very happy to own a bottle.
